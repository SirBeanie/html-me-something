About me and comic books

When did I start collecting?

I started collecting comic books when I was a little kid, sometime around 6 or 7 years old. It probably caught
my attentions so much, because me and my dad would always go to comic book shows togather. I even
remember going to peoples houses and looking through rooms full of comic books. I have been a collector ever since.

What are my favorite books?

Collecting books over the years I have acquired many favorites. The main book that the main book that always
comes to mind is Amazing Fantasy 15, which is the 1st appearance of SpiderMan. My second favorite book
would be Incredible Hulk 181, which is the 1st appearance of Wolverine. My third favorite book would be
Action Comics 1, which would be the 1st appearance of Superman.

How do you collect today?

Today I am still collecting as much as before. I have a huge collection with some pretty key comics from
the gold to copper ages. I am actually hoping to build an online site I can use to trade comics and other collectables.
